## What our supporters contribute

- **Western Digital** — equipment and expertise for AI research, including JBOD/JBOF storage for AI-PHI infrastructure
- **Volpara Health** — equipment for breast-density calculation supporting HIPIMR research
- **iCAD** — expertise and equipment for breast studies including the 3CB study and HIPIMR
- **GE Healthcare** — funding for investigator-initiated studies
- **SizeStream** — in-kind equipment and technical expertise for body-composition studies
- **Hologic** — equipment for NIH-funded studies including contrast-enhanced mammography systems; grant support for the Da Kine Study
- **Fit3D** — in-kind equipment and technical expertise for Shape Up! data collection
- **NVIDIA** — technical expertise and academic pricing for AI research
- **Styku** — in-kind equipment and technical expertise
- **Sutter Health**, **American Cancer Society**, **Delphinus MT**, **Densitas Health**, **MagView**, **Myriad Genetics** — additional supporting organizations
